Cipher / Brand Mark Exploration

Four Directions for the Mark

Each concept draws from the Signal visual identity. React to the direction and energy, not pixel perfection — a designer will refine whichever direction resonates.

01 Signal Resolve

A waveform that transitions from noise (chaotic) to signal (clean). The core Cipher metaphor in a single mark — finding the pattern in chaos. The clean wave emerges from static.

Design Notes

The mark tells the brand story: noise becomes signal. The blue accent dot marks the peak — the moment of clarity. Works as a standalone mark or paired with the wordmark. Could animate beautifully (noise draws first, then resolves into clean wave).

02 Pulse C

The letter C formed by a heartbeat/signal pulse. Combines the brand initial with vital signs energy. The sharp spike gives it edge — not a soft wellness curve.

Design Notes

Lettermark that doubles as a health signal. The open C with pulse spikes at both endpoints gives it life and energy — not a static letter. Red dot (pulse/alive) at top, green dot (health/growth) at bottom. Immediately reads as health + data without being clinical.

03 Decode Bars

Vertical frequency bars — like a spectrum analyzer or barcode — that suggest data being read and interpreted. The bars use the brand colors as channels. Feels technical, alive, and codified.

Design Notes

Spectrum/barcode energy — data being decoded. Each colored bar represents a health channel (blue = data/intelligence, green = health/growth, red = energy/pulse). The muted bars are noise; the colored ones are signal. Has a code-breaking quality that maps directly to the name. Could animate as bars rising and falling like a live readout.

04 Scan Dot

A single point with radiating signal rings — like radar, sonar, or a scanning pulse finding a pattern. Minimal, iconic, scales to any size. The dot is the person; the rings are the data being decoded around them.
