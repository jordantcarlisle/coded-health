10 Recommendation

My Take

Direction A (Mono Bold) is the strongest fit for Cipher because:

  • Monospace = decoding. It's the typeface of terminals, code editors, data readouts — exactly the "cipher" metaphor
  • Bold weight gives authority without needing a symbol. It commands attention.
  • Moderate tracking keeps it readable at all sizes while maintaining technical character
  • Pairs naturally with the Inter tagline — the type contrast (mono + sans) IS the brand system

For the favicon/avatar: JetBrains Mono bold "C" in white on black (rounded square). Clean, scales perfectly, recognizable.

What to tell a designer: "Refine the letterspacing, explore minor custom touches to the letterforms (especially the C and H), and build out the full lockup system with tagline variants."
