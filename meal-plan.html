Weekly Meal Plan

Portfolio Diet + Mediterranean. Each day covers the five pillars: almonds, soy protein, viscous fiber, plant sterols, and EVOO. Fatty fish appears 3x (Mon, Fri, Sun).

Monday
Breakfast
Overnight oats — 1/2 cup rolled oats, soy milk, 2 tbsp ground flaxseed, 1 cup mixed berries, 1oz sliced almonds
Dinner
Baked salmon fillet with arugula salad (EVOO + lemon dressing), roasted sweet potato, 1 cup lentil soup on the side
Tuesday
Breakfast
Smoothie — soy milk, 1 cup spinach, 1 cup blueberries, 2 tbsp ground flaxseed, 1 tbsp almond butter, 1/2 banana
Dinner
Tofu stir-fry (firm tofu, broccoli, bell pepper, garlic, ginger) over barley, drizzled with EVOO. Side of kimchi.
Wednesday
Breakfast
Steel-cut oatmeal with walnuts, pomegranate seeds, and a drizzle of honey. Soy milk on the side.
Lunch
Big salad — arugula, chickpeas (1 cup), avocado, cherry tomatoes, roasted beets, pumpkin seeds, EVOO vinaigrette
Dinner
Sardines on sourdough toast with lemon + capers. Side of sauerkraut and steamed spinach.
Thursday
Breakfast
Overnight oats (batch from Monday — same recipe, grab from fridge)
Dinner
Black bean and sweet potato chili (1 cup beans, cumin, smoked paprika, tomatoes) topped with avocado. Side of mixed greens with EVOO.
Friday
Breakfast
Smoothie — soy milk, strawberries, 1/4 cup edamame, 2 tbsp ground flaxseed, 1 tbsp almond butter
Dinner
Pan-seared mackerel fillets in EVOO. Roasted cauliflower and Brussels sprouts. Quinoa with lemon-tahini drizzle.
Saturday
Breakfast
Tempeh scramble (crumbled tempeh, turmeric, spinach, bell pepper, onion) with sourdough. 8oz pomegranate juice.
Lunch
Mediterranean bowl — farro, hummus, cucumber, tomato, olives, arugula, small amount of feta, EVOO
Dinner
Miso-glazed tofu over soba noodles with bok choy and edamame
Sunday
Breakfast
Oatmeal + ground flaxseed + almonds + berries (same foundation as Monday)
Dinner
Salmon fillet, roasted beet and lentil salad with goat cheese, walnuts, balsamic-EVOO dressing. Side of natto.

Daily Constants

  • Morning: Psyllium husk (5g) in water + Omega-3 + plant sterols with breakfast
  • Snack: 23 almonds (1 oz) — keep a bag at your desk
  • Evening: Psyllium husk (5g) in water + Magnesium glycinate + K2 + CoQ10 with dinner
  • Cook with: EVOO as your default oil (2+ tbsp/day)
  • Drink: Pomegranate juice (8oz) or green tea instead of afternoon coffee
